Assets for a castlevania-like platformer I mocked up quite some time
ago.  Stiff and kinda crappy animation on the main character.  Includes
tiles, two parallax backgrounds, a main character, and a really
badly-done bat.  Colors on the exterior environment could use some love,
but that was one of a whole bunch of things I didn't have the time to
address.

Two screenshots showing what you can do with it.
